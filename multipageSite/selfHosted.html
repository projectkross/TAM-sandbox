What's a self-hosted agent?

These are the same pendo.js files that all browsers download when a snippet is properly installed. The differences are:

  • They are hosted on the client's servers
  • They do not update automatically
How can you tell?

Many times, the contact at the client may not know if they self-host. You can check a few different ways.

  • Check the url in the snippet.
  • Look at a meta event for "selfHosted": true
  • Compare pendo.VERSION to the agent version in the UI.
